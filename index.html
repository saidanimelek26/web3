Phone Information Reader

Access device information using AT commands via WebUSB

About This Tool

This tool attempts to communicate with your phone using the WebUSB API to execute AT commands. It works best with devices that expose a modem interface via USB without requiring additional drivers or ADB.

Connection Instructions

  • Connect your phone to your computer using a USB cable
  • Enable USB debugging on your Android device (Developer options)
  • On your phone, select "File Transfer" or "MTP" mode when the USB prompt appears
  • Some devices may require you to enable "USB tethering" temporarily
  • Click "Connect to Device" and select your phone from the list

Note: Browser support for WebUSB is limited. This works best in Chrome/Edge on desktop.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues:

  • Try a different USB cable or port
  • Check if your device manufacturer requires special drivers
  • Restart your phone and computer
  • Try enabling/disabling USB tethering
  • Some devices require specific USB modes (PTP, RNDIS, etc.)
